Henri Selmer RSA Truvoice Model TV10 Mid 1950’s

This is an incredibly rare amp – from the first range of Selmer Guitar amps  Two tone Crocodile Skin with 10 inch Goodmans speaker. 10 watt amp set in base. Bakelite handle. Amp has been recapped  by John Chambers the UK’s leading expert on vintage valve amp repairs – see photos. Uses 19AQ5 Output Tubes. Same DC Design as the TV15 with the same requirements to use with an isolating transformer Some staining and wear on the covering and no back, but good condition for its age.
